UAA Engineering & Industry Building

A new 81,500 sf facility will house engineering laboratory and teaching areas not currently available on campus. The project includes: communication labs, electrical engineering labs, fluid labs, heat and mass transfer labs, foundation engineering, transportation and highway engineering, land surveying, machine shop, wood shop, service yard, and conferencing/collaborative learning areas.
